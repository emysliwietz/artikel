* Fonts
** Definition Overview
Fontspec defines:
- BoldFont \textbf \strong
- ItalicFont \textit \emph
- BoldItalicFont
- SlantedFont \textsl
- BoldSlantedFont
- SwashFont \textsw
- BoldSwashFont
- SmallCapsFont \textsc
- UprightFont
FontFace defines
- xl: Extra Light \textxl
- l: Light \textl
- (Regular)
- md: Medium \textmd
- sb: Semibold \textsb
- db: Demibold \textdb
- (Bold) \textbf
- k: Black \textbl \textk
